Here's what was unveiled at E3 2018:
Nintendo
- Revealed SUper Smash Bros. Ultimate, the lastest Smash Bros. game with all of the fighters and characters from past entries.
- Unvelied Super Mario Party, the lastest entry in the long-running Mario Party series
- Gave more details on Pokemon Let's Go Pikachu and Eevee, the lastest entries in the Pokemon series
Sony
- Revealed Destiny 2, the sequel to the original Destiny game.
- Got a deeper dive into Death Stranding, the lastest game by director Hideo Kojima
Microsoft
- Ori and the Will of the Wisps was announced
- The worst Fallout game, Fallout 76, was unveiled
- a new in-depth look at Kingdom Hearts 3
