Here's what was unveiled at E3 2010:
Nintendo
- the CEO of Nintendo, Satoru Iwata, unveiled the 3DS, including that it had a microSD card slot and that it could play 3D movies.
- Showed off the lastest entries in the Super Mario platformer series and the Mario Kart franchise.
- Unveiled Donkey Kong Country Returns, the lastest entry in the Donkey Kong Country series.
Sony
- Revealed more info on the PlayStation Move, and announced the release dates in various reigons, and a few of its games.
-Unveiled PlayStation Plus, which allowed users to get discounts on PlayStation Store games, and have free access to some games.
Microsoft
- Fully unveiled the Kinect hardware, which allowed the Xbox to track player's bodies so that they can be more immersed in games
- Announced Xbox games such as Call of Duty: Black Ops, Portal 2, and Halo Reach.
