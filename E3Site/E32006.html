Here's what was unveiled at E3 2006:
Nintendo
- Showed off the Wii and many of it's games, including Super Mario Galaxy, Metroid Prime 3, and Wii Sports.
- Revealed the Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess' Wii port, including it's widescreen visuals and motion controls
- Unveiled the DS Lite, the smaller, slimmer version of the original Nintendo DS.
Sony
- Revealed the PlayStation 3, that it could play CDs and DVDs, and that it would be able to output 1080p gameplay, which wasn't something any other game console had.
-Revealed the first Uncharted game, and the overall Uncharted series sold 18 million games on the PS3 alone
Microsoft
- Some big Xbox 360 games were revealed, including Mass Effect and Bioshock
- Announced many new Xbox Live games, including Contra, Frogger, and Pac-Man
